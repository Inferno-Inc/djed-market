Javascript Engineer

Really into family? Really into social apps and productivity apps? Join the Djed Javascript engineering team.

The platform is designed to become a hub for family life, gathering data such as contacts, calendars, notes, photos, health information and shopping lists into one place. Each family can build a "tribe" – a network of trusted family and friends – to share the information with.

Djed, the family app, is looking for cool, motivated, and talented Javascript engineers to work full time in downtown Austin, TX. Djed is building a revolutionary social productivity app for your family and your extended family: a private network for your "Tribe."

Join a company of 14 creative people. Work with talented designers, iOS engineers and back-end engineers on a beautiful and engaging app for families.


Key Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s or greater in computer science or equivalent experience.
  • Strong object-oriented and functional design skills.
  • Strong knowledge of Javascript MVC frameworks, especially Backbone.
  • Working knowledge of CSS3
  • 3+ years of professional software development experience.
  • Passion for designing and implementing an outstanding consumer application

Skills

  • Effective client-side modeling of RESTful APIs
  • Functional javascript programming with lodash/underscore
  • Javascript build systems, especially browserify and gulp
  • Unit and functional javascript testing
  • Github workflow
  • Strong attention to detail.
  • Excellent communication skills is a must.

About Djed Studios

We are located in Downtown Austin, TX, in a great old building - tall ceilings, brick walls, front-facing windows with natural light.  Everyone gets Aeron Chairs, MacBook Pros, plenty of snacks and drinks, and paid parking.  We are developing a Family App, so we understand and appreciate what it means to have a family - that is our core.  We launched the responsive webapp at SXSW interactive and have just started a large beta test. Our CEO just presented at SXSW Interactive and we had 4 days of SXSW Open House events showing off the Web Application to the world.  Our Creative Director has an amazing design and vision for the responsive javascript app.

Salary and benefits commensurate with experience and the industry.  Please email us your resume at jobs@djed.com.
