Djed Inc. To Beta Launch Revolutionary New Family App During SXSW March 13

At Djed here's how we think about privacy:

Family privacy means I want my family to be left alone, not advertised to, not manipulated as part of an online experiment. It means I don’t want my family’s data sold to a third party.

We're created an application that your family controls. You decide who sees medication information, notes about parent-teacher meetings, or photos and videos about sports and dance recitals and trips to Costa Rica.

We've created a tool that allows us to move the information off our computers and phones and dozens of other places and into a safe cloud infrastructure hosted by the world's leading provider, Amazon Web Services.

In exchange for this Djed will let families try out the product for free for a while, then ask families to pay a small subscription every month, but give families a way to cancel anytime if we aren't the right solution.
